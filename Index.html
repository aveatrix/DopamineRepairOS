HOW DOPAMINE BREAKS
Dopamine isn't about pleasure — it's about anticipation, motivation, and drive. When you repeatedly expose yourself to high-stimulation inputs (social media, junk food, pornography), your brain responds by downregulating receptors — removing them to compensate for excess stimulation. The result: ordinary life feels flat. Nothing moves you.

The fix isn't medication. It's systematic receptor restoration through behavior.
